To Autumn

Golden leaves of autumn

danced and drifted to the ground.

They dotted the picturesque view of mother nature,

when the color of gold conquered the world.

Leaves are leaving,

but I’m not.

My hand holds yours,

your hand fits mine.

By chance,

my eyes meet thine.
