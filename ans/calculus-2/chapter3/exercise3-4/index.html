    习题3.4

    \subsection{习题3.4解答}

    \paragraph{第3题}

    利用三重积分的几何意义, 求下列三重积分的值.

    (1) \(\iiint\limits_{\Omega}1\D{x}\D{y}\D{z}, \Omega = \{(x, y, z) | \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \le z \le H\}\);

    (2) \(\iiint\limits_{\Omega}1\D{x}\D{y}\D{z}, \Omega = \{(x, y, z) | 0 \le z \le 1 - x - y, 0 \le y \le 1 - x, 0 \le x \le 1\}\).

    \paragraph{解}

    (1) 由题知, 这个积分的值是一个底面半径为 \(H\) 高为 \(H\) 的圆锥的体积, 因此为 \(\dfrac{1}{3}\pi H^3\).

    (2) 由题知, 这个积分的值是一个由三条两两垂直的长为 \(1\) 的边构成的正四面体的体积, 因此为 \(\dfrac{1}{6}\).

    \paragraph{第4题}

    将三重积分 \(\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(x, y, z)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z}\) 化为直角坐标下的累次积分.

    (1) \(\Omega = \{(x, y, z) | \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \le z \le 1\}\);

    (2) \(\Omega = \{(x, y, z) | 0 \le z \le x^2 + y^2, x + y \le 1, x \ge 0, y \ge 0\}\).

    \paragraph{解}

    (1) 由 \(\sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \le 1\) 得 \(-\sqrt{1-x^2} \le y \le \sqrt{1-x^2}\). 因此 \(\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(x, y, z)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} = \int_{0}^{1}\D{x}\int_{-\sqrt{1-x^2}}^{\sqrt{1-x^2}}\D{y}\int_{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}^{1}f(x, y, z)\D{z}\).

    (2) 由 \(x + y \le 1, x \ge 0, y \ge 0\) 得到 \(1 - x \le y \le 1, 0 \le x \le 1\). 因此 \(\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(x, y, z)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} = \int_{0}^{1}\D{x}\int_{1-x}^{1}\D{y}\int_{0}^{x^2+y^2}f(x, y, z)\D{z}\).

    \paragraph{第5题}

    计算下列三重积分的值.

    (1) \(\iiint\limits_{\Omega}xy^2z^3\D{x}\D{y}\D{z}\), \(\Omega\) 是由马鞍面 \(z = xy\) 与平面 \(y = x, x = 1, z = 0\) 所围成的空间区域;

    (3) \(\iiint\limits_{\Omega}x\cos(y + z)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z}\), \(\Omega\) 是由曲面 \(x = \sqrt{y}\) 与平面 \(x = 0, z = 0, y + z = \dfrac{\pi}{2}\) 围成的区域.

    \paragraph{解}

    (1) 由题知 \(\Omega = \{(x, y, z) | 0 \le x \le 1, 0 \le y \le x, 0 \le z \le xy\}\). 因此

    \[ \begin{aligned} \iiint\limits_{\Omega}xy^2z^3\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} & = \int_{0}^{1}\D{x}\int_{0}^{x}\D{y}\int_{0}^{xy}xy^2z^3\D{z} \\ & = \int_{0}^{1}\D{x}\int_{0}^{x}\dfrac{1}{4}x^5y^6\D{y} \\ & = \int_{0}^{1}\dfrac{1}{28}x^{12}\D{x} \\ & = \dfrac{1}{364} \end{aligned} \]

    (3) 由题知 \(\Omega = \{(x, y, z) | 0 \le z \le \dfrac{\pi}{2}, 0 \le y \le \dfrac{\pi}{2} - z, 0 \le x \le \sqrt{y}\}\). 因此

    \[ \begin{aligned} \iiint\limits_{\Omega}x\cos(y + z)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} & = \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\D{z}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}-z}\D{y}\int_{0}^{\sqrt{y}}x\cos(y+z)\D{x} \\ & = \dfrac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\D{z}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}-z}y\cos(y+z)\D{y} \\ & = \dfrac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2} - z - \cos z\right)\D{z} \\ & = \dfrac{\pi^2}{16} - \dfrac{1}{2} \end{aligned} \]

    \paragraph{第6题}

    计算累次积分 \(I = \int_{0}^{1}\D{x}\int_{0}^{x}\D{y}\int_{0}^{y}\dfrac{\cos z}{(1 - z)^2}\D{z}\) 的值.

    \paragraph{解}

    积分区域为 \(\Omega = \{0 \le z \le y \le x \le 1\}\). 因此

    \[ \begin{aligned} I & = \int_{0}^{1}\D{x}\int_{0}^{x}\D{y}\int_{0}^{y}\dfrac{\cos z}{(1 - z)^2}\D{z} \\ & = \int_{0}^{1}\D{z}\int_{z}^{1}\D{y}\int_{y}^{1}\dfrac{\cos z}{(1-z)^2}\D{x} \\ & = \int_{0}^{1}\D{z}\int_{z}^{1}(1-y)\dfrac{\cos z}{(1-z)^2}\D{y} \\ & = \dfrac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{1}\cos z\D{z} \\ & = \dfrac{\sin 1}{2} \end{aligned} \]

    \paragraph{第7题}

    计算下列三重积分的值.

    (1) \(\iiint\limits_{\Omega}\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}\D{x}\D{y}\D{z}, \Omega = \{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \le z \le 1\}\);

    (3) \(\iiint\limits_{\Omega}\dfrac{z}{x^2 + y^2}\D{x}\D{y}\D{z}, \Omega = \{(x, y, z) | 0 \le z \le x^2 + y^2, x + y \le 1, x, y \ge 0\}\);

    (5) \(\iiint\limits_{\Omega}xyz\D{x}\D{y}\D{z}, \Omega = \{(x, y, z) | x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \le 4, x^2 + y^2 + (z - 2)^2\le 4, x \ge 0, y \ge 0\}\);

    \paragraph{解}

    (1) 做坐标变换 \(\begin{cases}x = r\cos \theta, \\ y = r\sin \theta, \\ z = z,\end{cases}\) 则

    \[ \begin{aligned} \iiint\limits_{\Omega}\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} & = \int_{0}^{1}\D{r}\int_{0}^{2\pi}\D{\theta}\int_{r}^{1}r^2\D{z} \\ & = \int_{0}^{1}\D{r}\int_{0}^{2\pi}r^2(1-r)\D{\theta} \\ & = 2\pi\int_{0}^{1}r^2(1-r)\D{r} \\ & = \dfrac{\pi}{6} \end{aligned} \]

    (3)

    \[ \begin{aligned} \iiint\limits_{\Omega}\dfrac{z}{x^2 + y^2}\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} & = \int_{0}^{1}\D{x}\int_{0}^{1-x}\D{y}\int_{0}^{x^2+y^2}\dfrac{z}{x^2 + y^2}\D{z} \\ & = \dfrac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{1}\D{x}\int_{0}^{1-x}(x^2+y^2)\D{y} \\ & = \dfrac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{1}\left(x^2(1-x) + \dfrac{1}{3}(1-x)^3\right)\D{x} \\ & = \dfrac{1}{12} \end{aligned} \]

    (5)

    做坐标变换 \(\begin{cases}x = r\sin\varphi\cos\theta, \\ y = r\sin\varphi\sin\theta, \\ z = r\cos\varphi, \end{cases}\) 则 \(\Omega = \Omega_1 + \Omega_2\), 其中

    \[ \begin{aligned} \Omega_1 & = \{0 \le r \le 2, 0 \le \varphi \le \dfrac{\pi}{4}, 0 \le \theta \le \dfrac{\pi}{2}\}, \\ \Omega_2 & = \{0 \le r \le 4\cos\varphi, \dfrac{\pi}{4} \le \varphi \le \dfrac{\pi}{2}, 0 \le \theta \le \dfrac{\pi}{2}\}. \end{aligned} \]

    因此

    \[ \begin{aligned} \iiint\limits_{\Omega_1}xyz\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} & = \int_{0}^{2}r^5\D{r}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\sin^3\varphi \cos\varphi\D{\varphi}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin\theta\cos\theta\D{\theta} \\ & = \int_{0}^{2}r^5\D{r}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\sin^3\varphi \D{\sin\varphi}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin\theta\D{\sin\theta} \\ & = \dfrac{32}{3}\cdot\dfrac{1}{16}\cdot\dfrac{1}{2} \\ & = \dfrac{1}{3} \end{aligned} \]

    且

    \[ \begin{aligned} \iiint\limits_{\Omega_2}xyz\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} & = \int_{\frac{\pi}{4}}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin^3\varphi \cos\varphi\D{\varphi}\int_{0}^{4\cos\varphi}r^5\D{r}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin\theta\cos\theta\D{\theta} \\ & = \dfrac{1}{2}\int_{\frac{\pi}{4}}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin^3\varphi \cos\varphi\D{\varphi}\int_{0}^{4\cos\varphi}r^5\D{r} \\ & = \dfrac{1024}{3}\int_{\frac{\pi}{4}}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin^3\varphi\cos^7\varphi\D{\varphi} \\ & = -\dfrac{1024}{3}\int_{\frac{\pi}{4}}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}(1-\cos^2\varphi)\cos^7\varphi\D{\cos\varphi} \\ & = \dfrac{1024}{3}\int_{0}^{\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}(u^7-u^9)\D{u} \\ & = \dfrac{8}{5} \end{aligned} \]

    故

    \[ \begin{aligned} \iiint\limits_{\Omega}xyz\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} & = \iiint\limits_{\Omega_1}xyz\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} + \iiint\limits_{\Omega_2}xyz\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} \\ & = \dfrac{1}{3} + \dfrac{8}{5} \\ & = \dfrac{29}{15} \end{aligned} \]

    \paragraph{第8题}

    做适当的变量代换, 计算下列三重积分.

    (1) \(\iiint\limits_{\Omega}\sqrt{1 - \dfrac{x^2}{a^2} - \dfrac{y^2}{b^2} - \dfrac{z^2}{c^2}}\D{x}\D{y}\D{z}, \Omega = \{(x, y, z) | \dfrac{x^2}{a^2} + \dfrac{y^2}{b^2} + \dfrac{z^2}{c^2} \le 1\}\);

    \paragraph{解}

    (1) 作代换 \(\begin{cases}x = ar\sin\varphi\cos\theta, \\ y = br\sin\varphi\sin\theta, \\ z = cr\sin\varphi,\end{cases}\) 有

    \[ \begin{aligned} \iiint\limits_{\Omega}\sqrt{1 - \dfrac{x^2}{a^2} - \dfrac{y^2}{b^2} - \dfrac{z^2}{c^2}}\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} & = abc\int_{0}^{2\pi}\D{\theta}\int_{0}^{\pi}\sin\varphi\D{\varphi}\int_{0}^{1}\sqrt{1-r^2}r^2\D{r} \\ & = 4\pi abc\int_{0}^{1}r^2\sqrt{1-r^2}\D{r} \\ & = 4\pi abc\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin^2\alpha\cos\alpha\D{\sin\alpha} \\ & = 4\pi abc \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}(\sin^2\alpha - \sin^4\alpha)\D{\alpha} \\ & = 4\pi abc\cdot (\dfrac{1}{2} - \dfrac{3\cdot 1}{4\cdot 2})\cdot\dfrac{\pi}{2} \\ & = \dfrac{\pi^2 abc}{4} \end{aligned} \]

    \paragraph{第10题}

    设 \(f(t)\) 在 \((-\infty, +\infty)\) 上连续, \(f(t) = 3\iiint\limits_{x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \le t^2}f(\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2})\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} + \left|t^3\right|\), 求 \(f(t)\).

    \paragraph{解}

    注意到 \(f(t)\) 为偶函数, 因此暂时先考虑 \(t\ge 0\) 时的情况, 此时 \(f(t) = 3\iiint\limits_{x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \le t^2}f(\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2})\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} + t^3\). 作变换 \(\begin{cases}x = r\sin\varphi\cos\theta, \\ y = r\sin\varphi\sin\theta, \\ z = r\sin\varphi,\end{cases}\), 则

    \[ \begin{aligned} f(t) & = 3\iiint\limits_{x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \le t^2}f(\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2})\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} + t^3 \\ & = t^3 + \int_{0}^{\pi}\sin\varphi\D{\varphi}\int_{0}^{2\pi}\D{\theta}\int_{-t}^{t}r^2f(r)\D{r} \\ & = t^3 + 12 \pi \int_{-t}^{t}r^2f(r)\D{r} \\ & = t^3 + 24 \pi \int_{0}^{t}r^2f(r)\D{r} \end{aligned} \]

    对等式左右两侧求导, 有 \(f'(t) = 3t^2 + t^2f(t)\). 解得 \(f(t) = Ce^{8\pi t^3}-\dfrac{1}{8\pi}(t\ge 0)\). 又因为 \(f(0) = 0\) 且 \(f(t)\) 为偶函数, 因此

    \[ f(t) = \dfrac{1}{8\pi}e^{8\pi \left|t^3\right|}-\dfrac{1}{8\pi} \]

    \paragraph{第11题}

    函数 \(f(x, y, z)\) 连续, 计算极限 \(\lim\limits_{r \to 0^{+}}\dfrac{1}{r^3}\iiint\limits_{x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \le r^2}f(x, y, z)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z}\).

    \paragraph{解}

    将积分中值定理推广到三维, 有 \(\exists \xi, \eta, \zeta \in (0, r)\) 使得 \(\iiint\limits_{x^2+y^2+z^2\le r^2}f(x, y, z)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} = \dfrac{4}{3}\pi r^3f(\xi, \eta, \zeta)\). 代入则有

    \[ \begin{aligned} \lim\limits_{r \to 0^{+}}\dfrac{1}{r^3}\iiint\limits_{x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \le r^2}f(x, y, z)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} & = \lim\limits_{r \to 0^{+}}\dfrac{1}{r^3}\dfrac{4}{3}\pi r^3f(\xi, \eta, \zeta) \quad \xi, \eta, \zeta \in (0, r) \\ & = \dfrac{4}{3}\pi f(0, 0, 0) \end{aligned} \]
