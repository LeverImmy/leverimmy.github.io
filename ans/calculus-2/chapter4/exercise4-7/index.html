    习题4.7

    \subsection{习题4.7解答}

    \paragraph{第2题}

    计算曲面积分 \(\oint\limits_{S^{+}}\dfrac{x\D{y}\wedge\D{z} + y\D{z}\wedge\D{x} + z\D{x}\wedge\D{y}}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}}\), 其中 \(S^{+}\) 为: % 这里符号有问题, 应该是 \oiint

    (1) 不包含也不经过原点的半径为 \(R\) 的球面外侧;

    (2) 不包含也不经过原点的任意封闭曲面的外侧;

    (3) 球面 \(x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = \epsilon^2(\epsilon > 0)\);

    (4) 包含原点在其内部的任意封闭曲面的外侧.

    \paragraph{解}

    \(X = \dfrac{x}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}}, Y = \dfrac{y}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}}, Z = \dfrac{z}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}}\). 因此 \(\dfrac{\partial X}{\partial x} = \dfrac{y^2 + z^2 - 2x^2}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^{\frac{5}{2}}}, \dfrac{\partial Y}{\partial y} = \dfrac{x^2 + z^2 - 2y^2}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^{\frac{5}{2}}}, \dfrac{\partial Z}{\partial z} = \dfrac{x^2 + y^2 - 2z^2}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^{\frac{5}{2}}}\).

    (1) 由 Gauss 公式, 该积分值为 \(0\).

    (2) 由 Gauss 公式, 该积分值为 \(0\).

    (3) 设此球面为 \(\Gamma\), 该积分值为 \(J\). 由对称性知 \(J = 3\oint\limits_{\Gamma^{+}}Z\D{x}\wedge\D{y}\). 对于 \(z > 0\), \(\D{x}\wedge\D{y} = \D{x}\D{y}, z = \sqrt{\epsilon^2 - x^2 - y^2}\). 对于 \(z < 0\), \(\D{x}\wedge\D{y} = -\D{x}\D{y}, z = -\sqrt{\epsilon^2 - x^2 - y^2}\). 因此

    \[ \begin{aligned} J & = \dfrac{6}{\epsilon^3}\iint\limits_{D}\sqrt{\epsilon^2 - x^2 - y^2}\D{x}\D{y} \\ & = \dfrac{6}{\epsilon^3}\int_{0}^{\epsilon}r\sqrt{\epsilon^2 - r^2}\D{r}\int_{0}^{2\pi}\D{\theta} \\ & = 2\pi \cdot\dfrac{9}{2}(\epsilon^2 - r^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}\bigg\vert_{0}^{\epsilon} \\ & = 4\pi \end{aligned} \]

    (4) 设积分区域为 \(A\), 则由 Gauss 公式, \(\oint\limits_{\Gamma^{-}\cup A^{+}}\dfrac{x\D{y}\wedge\D{z} + y\D{z}\wedge\D{x} + z\D{x}\wedge\D{y}}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}} = 0\). 因此 % 这里符号有问题, 应该是 \oiint

    \[ \begin{aligned} \quad & \oint\limits_{A^{+}}\dfrac{x\D{y}\wedge\D{z} + y\D{z}\wedge\D{x} + z\D{x}\wedge\D{y}}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}} \\ % 这里符号有问题, 应该是 \oiint & = -\oint\limits_{\Gamma^{-}}\dfrac{x\D{y}\wedge\D{z} + y\D{z}\wedge\D{x} + z\D{x}\wedge\D{y}}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}} \\ % 这里符号有问题, 应该是 \oiint & = \oint\limits_{\Gamma^{+}}\dfrac{x\D{y}\wedge\D{z} + y\D{z}\wedge\D{x} + z\D{x}\wedge\D{y}}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}} \\ % 这里符号有问题, 应该是 \oiint & = 4\pi \end{aligned} \]

    \paragraph{第3题}

    利用 Gauss 公式计算下列曲面积分.

    (1) \(\oint\limits_{S^{+}}x^3\D{y}\wedge\D{z} + y^3\D{z}\wedge\D{x} + z^3\D{x}\wedge\D{y}\), 其中 \(S^{+}\) 为球面 \(x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = a^2(a > 0)\) 的外侧; % 这里符号有问题, 应该是 \oiint

    (3) \(\oint\limits_{S^{+}}(x + 2y + 3z)\D{x}\wedge\D{y} + (y+2z)\D{y}\wedge\D{z} + (z^2 - 1)\D{z}\wedge\D{x}\), 其中 \(S^{+}\) 为平面 \(x + y + z = 1\) 及与三个坐标平面所围四面体的表面, 外侧为正; % 这里符号有问题, 应该是 \oiint

    (5) \(\oint\limits_{S^{+}}\vct{A}\cdot\D{\vct{S}}\), 其中 \(\vct{A} = \dfrac{\vct{r}}{r^3}, \vct{r} = x\vct{i} + y\vct{j} + z\vct{k}, r = ||\vct{r}||\), \(S\) 为椭球面 \(\dfrac{x^2}{a^2} + \dfrac{y^2}{b^2} + \dfrac{z^2}{c^2} = 1\), 外侧为正; % 这里符号有问题, 应该是 \oiint

    \paragraph{解}

    (1) \(X = x^3, Y = y^3, Z = z^3\), 且 \(x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = R^2\). 由 Gauss 公式

    \[ \begin{aligned} \oint\limits_{S^{+}}\vct{A}\cdot\D{\vct{S}} & = \iiint\limits_{\Omega}\left(\dfrac{\partial X}{\partial x} + \dfrac{\partial Y}{\partial y} + \dfrac{\partial Z}{\partial z}\right)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} \\ % 这里符号有问题, 应该是 \oiint & = \iiint\limits_{\Omega}3R^2\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} \\ & = 4\pi \end{aligned} \]

    (3) \(X = x + 2y + 3z, Y = y + 2z, Z = z^2 - 1\). 由 Gauss 公式

    \[ \begin{aligned} \quad & \oint\limits_{S^{+}}(x + 2y + 3z)\D{x}\wedge\D{y} + (y+2z)\D{y}\wedge\D{z} + (z^2 - 1)\D{z}\wedge\D{x} \\ % 这里符号有问题, 应该是 \oiint & = \iiint\limits_{\Omega}\left(\dfrac{\partial X}{\partial x} + \dfrac{\partial Y}{\partial y} + \dfrac{\partial Z}{\partial z}\right)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} \\ & = \iiint\limits_{\Omega}(0 + 0 + 3)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} \\ & = \dfrac{1}{2} \end{aligned} \]

    (5) 由第2题 (4) 得 \(\oint\limits_{S^{+}}\vct{A}\cdot\D{\vct{S}} = 4\pi\). % 这里符号有问题, 应该是 \oiint

    \paragraph{第4题}

    求向量场 \(\vct{A}\) 通过闭曲面 \(S\) (从里向外) 的通量 \(\Phi = \oint\limits_{S^{+}}\vct{A}\cdot\D{\vct{S}}\).

    (1) \(\vct{A} = x^3\vct{i} + y^3\vct{j} + z^3\vct{k}\), \(S\) 为球面 \(x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = R^2\);

    (2) \(\vct{A} = (x - y + z)\vct{i} + (y - z + x)\vct{j} + (z - x + y)\vct{k}\), \(S\) 为椭球面 \(\dfrac{x^2}{a^2} + \dfrac{y^2}{b^2} + \dfrac{z^2}{c^2} = 1\).

    \paragraph{解}

    (1) 由第3题 (1) 得 \(\Phi = \dfrac{12}{5}\pi R^5\).

    (2) \(X = x - y - z, Y = y - z + x, Z = z - x + y\). 由 Gauss 公式

    \[ \begin{aligned} \Phi & = \oint\limits_{S^{+}}\vct{A}\cdot\D{\vct{S}} \\ & = \iiint\limits_{\Omega}\left(\dfrac{\partial X}{\partial x} + \dfrac{\partial Y}{\partial y} + \dfrac{\partial Z}{\partial z}\right)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} \\ & = \iiint\limits_{\Omega}(1 + 1 + 1)\D{x}\D{y}\D{z} \\ & = 4\pi abc \end{aligned} \]

    \paragraph{第5题}

    利用 Stokes 公式计算下列积分.

    (1) \(\oint_{L^{+}}y\D{x} + z\D{y} + x\D{z}\), 其中 \(L\) 是球面 \(x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = R^2\) 与平面 \(x + y + z = 0\) 的交线, 从 \(z\) 轴正向看上去, 为逆时针方向;

    (3) \(\oint_{L^{+}}y^2\D{x}+z^2\D{y} + x^2\D{z}\), 其中 \(L\) 是以 \(A(a, 0, 0), B(0, b, 0), C(0, 0, c)(a, b, c > 0)\) 为顶点的三角形的边界, 方向为 \(A \to B \to C \to A\).

    \paragraph{解}

    (1) 取 \(\vct{n} = \left(\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3}, \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3}, \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3}\right)\). 记 \(D\) 为球 \(x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \le R^2\) 被平面 \(x + y + z = 0\) 所截的截面. 由 Stokes 公式

    \[ \oint_{L^{+}}y\D{x} + z\D{y} + x\D{z} = \iint\limits_{D}(-1, -1, -1)\cdot\vct{n}\D{S} \]

    故 \(\oint_{L^{+}}y\D{x} + z\D{y} + x\D{z} = \sqrt{3}\iint\limits_{D}\D{S} = -\sqrt{3}\pi R^2\).

    (3) 由 Stokes 公式

    \[ \oint_{L^{+}}y^2\D{x} + z^2\D{y} + x^2\D{z} = \iint\limits_{S^{+}}-2z\D{y}\wedge\D{z} - 2x\D{z}\wedge\D{x} - 2y\D{x}\wedge\D{y} \]

    考虑其中的 \(\oint_{L^{+}}y^2\D{x} = \iint\limits_{S^{+}}2y\D{x}\wedge\D{y}\), 在 \(xy\) 平面内的投影 \(D_{xy} = \{(x, y) | 0 \le x \le a, 0 \le y \le b - \dfrac{b}{a}x\}\).

    \[ \begin{aligned} \oint_{L^{+}}y^2\D{x} & = \iint\limits_{D}2y\D{x}\D{y} \\ & = \int_{0}^{a}\D{x}\int_{0}^{b - \frac{b}{a}x}2y\D{y} \\ & = \int_{0}^{a}\left(b - \dfrac{b}{a}x\right)^2\D{x} \\ & = \dfrac{a}{3b}\left(\dfrac{b}{a}x - b\right)^3\bigg\vert_{0}^{a} \\ & = \dfrac{ab^2}{3} \end{aligned} \]

    由对称性知 \(\oint_{L^{+}}y^2\D{x}+z^2\D{y} + x^2\D{z} = \dfrac{ab^2 + bc^2 + ca^2}{3}\).

    \paragraph{第6题}

    试验证下列微分式为某个三元函数 \(u(x, y, z)\) 的全微分, 并求出该函数.

    (1) \(\dfrac{1}{x^2}(yz\D{x} - zx\D{y} - xy\D{z})\);

    (2) \(\dfrac{1}{(x + z)^2 + y^2}[y\D{x} - (z + x)\D{y} + y\D{z}]\).

    \paragraph{解}

    (1) 记 \(u(x, y, z) = -\dfrac{yz}{x} + C\), 则 \(\D{u} = \dfrac{1}{x^2}(yz\D{x} - zx\D{y} - xy\D{z})\).

    (2) 记 \(u(x, y, z) = \arctan{\dfrac{x + z}{y}} + C\), 则 \(\D{u} = \dfrac{1}{\left(\frac{x+z}{y}\right)^2 + 1}\D{\left(\dfrac{x + z}{y}\right)} = \dfrac{1}{(x + z)^2 + y^2}[y\D{x} - (z + x)\D{y} + y\D{z}]\).

    \paragraph{第7题}

    证明下列曲线积分与路径无关, 并求积分值.

    (1) \(\int_{(0, 0, 0)}^{(1, 2, 1)}(y + z)\D{x} + (z + x)\D{y} + (x + y)\D{z}\);

    (2) \(\int_{(1, -1, 1)}^{(1, 1, -1)}y^2z\D{x} + 2xyz\D{y} + xy^2\D{z}\);

    \paragraph{解}

    (1) 记 \(u(x, y, z) = xy + yz + zx\), 则 \(\int_{(0, 0, 0)}^{(1, 2, 1)}(y + z)\D{x} + (z + x)\D{y} + (x + y)\D{z} = u(1, 2, 1) - u(0, 0, 0) = 5\).

    (2) 记 \(u(x, y, z) = xy^2z\), 则 \(\int_{(1, -1, 1)}^{(1, 1, -1)}y^2z\D{x} + 2xyz\D{y} + xy^2\D{z} = u(1, 1, -1) - u(1, -1, 1) = -2\).

    \paragraph{第8题}

    求向量场 \(\vct{A} = \dfrac{-y\vct{i} + x\vct{j}}{x^2 + y^2} + a\vct{k}\) 沿下列曲线的环量 \(\Gamma = \oint_{L^{+}}\vct{A}\cdot\D{\vct{r}}\).

    (1) 圆周 \(\begin{cases}x^2 + y^2 = \epsilon^2, \\ z = 0, \end{cases}\epsilon > 0\);

    (2) 圆周 \(\begin{cases}(x-1)^2 + y^2 = r^2, \\ z = 2,\end{cases}r > 2\).

    \paragraph{解}

    (1) 做坐标变换 \(\begin{cases}x = \epsilon\cos\theta, \\ y = \epsilon\sin\theta, \end{cases}\) 则

    \[ \begin{aligned} \Gamma & = \oint_{L^{+}}\vct{A}\cdot\D{\vct{r}} \\ & = \int_{0}^{2\pi}\dfrac{-\epsilon\sin\theta\cdot(-\epsilon\sin\theta) + \epsilon\cos\theta\cdot\epsilon\cos\theta}{\epsilon^2}\D{\theta} \\ & = 2\pi \end{aligned} \]

    (2) \(X = \dfrac{-y}{x^2 + y^2}, Y = \dfrac{x}{x^2 + y^2}\). 设 (1) 中环路为 \(L_1\), 本题环路为 \(L_2\), \(L_1\) 与 \(L_2\) 构成的区域为 \(D\). 由于 \(\dfrac{\partial X}{\partial y} = \dfrac{y^2 - x^2}{(x^2 + y^2)^2} = \dfrac{\partial Y}{\partial x}\), 故由 Stokes 定理,

    \[ \begin{aligned} \oint_{L_1^{-}}\vct{A}\cdot\D{\vct{r}} + \oint_{L_2^{+}}\vct{A}\cdot\D{\vct{r}} = \iint\limits_{D}\left(\dfrac{\partial X}{\partial y} - \dfrac{\partial Y}{\partial x}\right)\D{x}\D{y} = 0 \end{aligned} \]

    故 \(\oint_{L_2^{+}}\vct{A}\cdot\D{\vct{r}} = -\oint_{L_1^{-}}\vct{A}\cdot\D{\vct{r}} = \oint_{L_1^{+}}\vct{A}\cdot\D{\vct{r}} = 2\pi\).

    \paragraph{第12题}

    若向量场的积分与路径无关, 就称向量场为保守场, 考察下列向量场是否为保守场, 如果是, 求出相应的势函数, 并计算积分 \(\int_{(4, 0, 1)}^{(2, 1, -1)}\vct{V}\cdot\D{\vct{r}}\):

    (1) \(\vct{V} = y\cos(xy)\vct{i} + x\cos(xy)\vct{j} + \sin z\vct{k}\);

    (3) \(\vct{V} = (6xy + z^2)\vct{i} + (3x^2 - z)\vct{j} + (3xz^2 - y)\vct{k}\);

    \paragraph{解}

    (1) \(X = y\cos(xy), Y = x\cos(xy), Z = \sin z\), 则

    \[ \begin{aligned} \rot{X, Y, Z} & = \begin{vmatrix}\vct{i} & \vct{j} & \vct{k} \\ \dfrac{\partial}{\partial x} & \dfrac{\partial}{\partial y} & \dfrac{\partial}{\partial z} \\ X & Y & Z\end{vmatrix} \\ & = 0 \end{aligned} \]

    因此该向量场是保守场, 且 \(u(x, y, z) = \sin(xy) - \cos z\). 故

    \[ \begin{aligned} \int_{(4, 0, 1)}^{(2, 1, -1)}\vct{V}\cdot\D{\vct{r}} & = u(2, 1, -1) - u(4, 0, 1) \\ & = \sin 2 \end{aligned} \]

    (3) \(X = 6xy + z^2, Y = 3x^2 - z, Z = 3xz^2 - y\), 则

    \[ \begin{aligned} \rot{X, Y, Z} & = \begin{vmatrix}\vct{i} & \vct{j} & \vct{k} \\ \dfrac{\partial}{\partial x} & \dfrac{\partial}{\partial y} & \dfrac{\partial}{\partial z} \\ X & Y & Z\end{vmatrix} \\ & = (2z - 3z^2)\vct{j} \\ & \neq 0 \end{aligned} \]

    因此该向量场不是保守场.
